## Territory

**In-lane:**
- website monitoring that goes beyond uptime
- website operations and proof loops
- technical SEO drift and Search Console measurement
- AI crawler / AI citation / AI visibility monitoring
- accessibility, security, performance, sitemap, robots, and metadata checks as part of site health
- client dashboard visibility and evidence-first reporting
- page experiments, landing-page measurement, and reader-satisfaction feedback loops

**Out-of-lane:**
- ADA lawsuit/compliance buyer education — belongs to ADA Audit Report
- emotional regulation / book content — belongs to The Participation Effect
- podiatry / medical patient education — belongs to Liddy Podiatry
- baby memory / milestone app content — belongs to Baby Milestone Journal
- Daily Rise companion-app practice content — Daily Rise is not a blog lane

## Lane guard

Every Site Clinic post should answer the operator question: what changed, what can be proven, what should be done next, and how Site Monitor will know if it worked.

Do not claim a page, pipeline, or deployment succeeded unless the relevant runtime proof exists. Do not call rankings successful when readers bounce immediately or GA4 engagement is weak.
